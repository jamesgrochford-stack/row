⚡ 3-Action Rule Pre-Match Protocol

Commit to 3 specific actions before kickoff — build confidence through clarity, not outcome.

1
Communicate Early
Talk to every defender in the first 5 minutes. Call for the ball. Set the tone as the organiser in front of the back four.
2
Demand the Ball
Show for every short pass from the keeper and CBs. Create and use the pocket. Don't hide — own the position.
3
Win First Duel
Compete to win the first physical contest. Set the physical standard early — it carries through the whole game.
